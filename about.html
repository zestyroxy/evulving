Evulving

In November 2018, I started writing a book as part of the yearly NaNoWriMo (National Novel Writing Month). The goal is to write 50,000 words in 30 days.

In the mornings, before writing, I would read the book Everyday Sexism by Laura Bates, created thanks to the awesome response her Everday Day Sexism Project It really reached me and I thought to myself "I've lived through 90% of this bullshirt!" (Yes, bullshirt is from Kristen Bell's character in The Good Place. Good catch, I like you already.)

I stopped everything I was doing and started writing an autobiography about all the moments I've lived through were sexism and my sex affected the situation. As it turns out, I have A LOT to say. Twenty days into writing my book, I realized this would actually be a great blog. So here we are.

Who are you?

My name is Roxanne and I am 31 years old. I am from Canada, now living in Germany.

What can I expect?

A blog post will be posted every Monday and Thursday of every week until I am out of things to say.

What will the posts be about?

The posts will be about my life. I thought it could help to gather all the moments that seem insignificant, but ultimately oppress us on a daily basis. There are so inequalities girls and women face throughout their lives; it needs to stop. Maybe if I share my entire experience, others will see how it runs deep.

How can I share my stories or comments?

I'm glad you asked! Share your experiences with me on Twitter using the hashtag #Evulving:

  1. Twitter Follow @NormalYouAre
  2. Share books/resources, findings, or personal experiences by email.
  3. Be respectful. This website and all it's related mediums are there to share personal experience and be supportive of one another. I aim for it to be a harassment-free environment for everyone, but I can't control everything.
